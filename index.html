Rock-paper-scissors is a simple hand game usually played by two people. The rules of the game are as follows:

1. Each player chooses one of three options: rock, paper, or scissors.

2. Rock beats scissors, because a rock can break a pair of scissors.

3. Scissors beats paper, because scissors can cut paper.

4. Paper beats rock, because a piece of paper can cover a rock.

5. If both players choose the same option, it is a tie and the game is replayed.

In short, rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. It's a game of chance, so there's no guaranteed way to win, but a player can try to guess what the other player will choose and choose the option that beats it.
